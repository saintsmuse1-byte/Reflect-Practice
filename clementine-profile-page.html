Clementine

Welcome! I'm Clementine, and I work with young people navigating the early stages of adulthood: students, early-career adults, or anyone feeling a bit unsure about their direction. My sessions are a calm, supportive space where you can talk through challenges, reflect on choices and life in general, and gain perspective on everyday life in a way that enables higher levels of confidence and a sense of agency.

About Me

As someone who has experienced the discomfort of leaving school to pursue online studies during the Covid-19 pandemic, leaving home for the first time to study far away as an 18 year old and is now about to engage myself in psychology honours (online, again!) as well as travelling overseas, I really understand the difficulties that one can face in coming to terms with different transitional challenges. I believe that having someone to hear your voice during the process can be a powerful tool to allow thriving and personal growth.

My Approach

My degree gave me a strong foundation in understanding human behaviour and thought processes, which informs the way I guide conversations, and support reflection. I have interests in Existential Psychology, as well as Identity and Narrative psychology, which influence my session structure and the way I interact and care for those around me.

I'm here to listen, provide space for reflection and help you feel less alone as you navigate life's small and big transitions. All sessions are online, relaxed and judgement free.
