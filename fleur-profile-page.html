About Me

I have lived and worked in the UK, France, Zimbabwe, and South Africa, experiences that have given me a broad cultural perspective and a deep respect for different life journeys. Alongside my personal roles as a mother, wife, and business owner, I bring a grounded, empathetic presence shaped by decades of navigating change, responsibility, and growth.

My Approach

My approach is calm, empathetic, and non-judgmental. I believe deeply in the importance of being heard and understood, especially during times of transition, uncertainty, or emotional overwhelm. I aim to provide a supportive space where clients can reflect, talk things through, and gain clarity at their own pace.

In addition to emotional support, I am able to support individuals who are considering starting their own business or who are already self-employed and feeling unsure, overwhelmed, or isolated. Having run my own business for many years, I understand the practical and emotional challenges of entrepreneurship and can offer thoughtful guidance, encouragement, and perspective.

My work is not about giving quick answers or telling people what to do, but about walking alongside them as they build confidence, resilience, and a clearer sense of direction.
